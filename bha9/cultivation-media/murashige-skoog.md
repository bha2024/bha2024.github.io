## Murashige Skoog medium

Murashige Skoog medium is used for plant micropropagation. Check the amounts per litre on the bottle.

|Compound| Amount | Unit |
|:-------|-------:|-----:|
|MS medium|4.4|g|
|Distilled water|up to 1000|mL|
